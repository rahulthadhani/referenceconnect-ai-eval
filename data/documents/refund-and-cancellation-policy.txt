Refund and Cancellation Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you are not satisfied within 30 days of your first payment, contact billing@company.com and we will issue a full refund. Refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days.

After 30 days, refunds are not available for monthly plans. Annual plans may be eligible for a prorated refund within 60 days — contact support to request one.

To cancel your subscription, go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan. Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.

You cannot pause a subscription. If you need to reduce costs temporarily, consider downgrading to the Starter free plan and upgrading again later. Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation.

Receipts are not required for refund requests — your account email is sufficient to locate your transaction.
