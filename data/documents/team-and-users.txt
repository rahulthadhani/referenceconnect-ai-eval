Team and Users

The Starter plan supports up to 3 users. The Pro plan supports unlimited users. Enterprise plans support unlimited users with advanced permission controls.

Multiple users can be logged in simultaneously from different devices. There is no concurrent session limit per user — you can be logged in on your phone, desktop, and browser at the same time.

To invite team members, go to Settings > Team > Invite Members and enter their email addresses. They will receive an invitation link valid for 7 days.

User roles: Owner (full access), Admin (manage members and billing), Member (standard access), and Viewer (read-only). Roles can be changed at any time by an Owner or Admin.

To update your billing information, go to Settings > Billing > Payment Method. You can update your credit card, billing address, and invoice email at any time.
