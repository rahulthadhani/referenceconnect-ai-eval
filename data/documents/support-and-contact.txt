Support and Contact

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm Eastern Time.

Live chat is available inside the app during support hours. Click the chat icon in the bottom right corner to start a conversation with a support agent.

Email support is available 24/7 at support@company.com. We respond to all emails within one business day. For urgent billing issues, include URGENT in the subject line.

Phone support is available on Enterprise plans only. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated support number in their welcome email.

You can also browse our help center at help.company.com for instant answers to common questions. The community forum at community.company.com is available for peer support at any time.
